First Death「初死」
Moments bleed onto one another Combat ennui, abhor Whilst unchained of fervor Galvanize self for more Unrelenting restlessness hither Crave and plunder, not implore Trials yet to pass, waiting in spite Distant murky contour Gashes disfiguring within Awaiting attainment, they sought Take all, deliver them Before the gluttony brings rot Sanguinary wings, spread apart Reaching out to heavens Embalming the unfortunate For deprivation of essence Penultimate construction upon us Screams and enmity, severed and drowned Transfigure it all to nil Rejuvenate anew their march of sound O’ Lost corporeal entities Repent for thy inert wickedness Judgment I bring upon thoust Embrace mine self-asserted holiness Pleas dying in discarnate ears Screams drawing first breath Counting minutes in seconds Plunging to their last death
