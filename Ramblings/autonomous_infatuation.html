Autonomous Infatuation
Body aching perpetually Short lasting flagellations Grasp my hand, cross my sight Blind sighted implications March onwards, Day n’ day Inscribe what transpired with might Singe mine own purity March forwards, Night n’ night Brisk ! A blinding Light Myopia present, yet despite Hark ! Serenity vivid to sight To my heart, brought respite Here I, engulfed in plight The hands extend aloft Scattered hopes and Haze ensues Gone that touch, O’ so soft ! Sensations never meant Providence shan’t not it resist Coalescence of Two Happen, may, but won’t persist Tenebrous clouds form once more Heart, blight enshrouded, I mourn Curse this anointed fate ! Sentiments, gone all but forlorn Tethered and severed Pavement batters each sole Dragging with shame Sallied and tainted soul March onwards, Day n’ day Inscribe what transpired in might Singe mine own purity March forwards, Night n’ night
